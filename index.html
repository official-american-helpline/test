How to Get Through the Automated System Faster

Here’s a proven method to reach a real agent at American Airlines without wasting 10 minutes:

  1. Call 1-888-669-8025
  2. When the system starts speaking, immediately say “agent”
  3. Repeat “agent” or “representative” until you're transferred
  4. If asked for a reason, say “customer service” or “existing reservation”
  5. Stay on the line—don’t hang up

Many travelers find this the fastest way to get real human help.
